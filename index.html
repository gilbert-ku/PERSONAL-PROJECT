Biography:

Early life and education.

Born in 1998 in Nairobi, Kenya. He grew up in a humble family of 8 siblings. Raised in Kibera Nairobi, later shift to up county when he was about 5years old. He went to masasba primary where he did his KCPE, later joined Musanda secondary school where he did his KCSE. Gilbert came back to Nairobi in 2018 with a purpose of looking for a job that can help him raise collage fees. He worked in construction site as his first job in the city. Apart from working, he went doing part time causes. First he did computer packages at Shofco and later did financial sales services at generation Kenya which introduced him to insurance industry. Further more, he did his executive certificate of proficiency in insurance, at collage of insurance. Gilbert was licensed as insurance agent by insurance regulatory authority, which helped him to work for different insurance company and letter he founder his insurance agency EBIA.
